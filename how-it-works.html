How it works.

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle We’ve all heard this way too often but have we done much about it?

Here’s your chance to help make a change in a better way while still earning points and getting a chance to attend some great Gigs in town or even other popular events.

As you recycle you earn points and these points build up and can be redeemed as coupons for discounts at outlets or stores and greater points add up to earn you those well deserved tickets to shows.
